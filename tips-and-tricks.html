Tips and Tricks

Here are some neat tips and tricks to help you bring CB some hardware.

 

  • Reaction time is key
    Sometimes, the difference between a win and a loss is decided on one toss-up, which is why it is imperative that you beat out the other team in buzzing in (provided you know the answer).

  • Listen for the "category" of the answer
    Usually the question specifies what "category" the answer belongs to, whether it be an author, painting, country, sports team, etc. You don't want to answer the name of an athlete when the question asked to name a sports team!
    For example: "This nation is home to ..."

  • Read the NAQT "You Gotta Know" Articles

  • Know your situation
    Change your playing style according to your team's current situation. For example, in tight games, perhaps taking a random guess will cost you the match, whereas doing the same when you have a comfortable lead is justifiable.

  • Have fun!
    This is the most clich√© "tip", but in all honesty there is no point in doing Quizbowl if you are not having fun doing it. Enjoy yourselves!
